AI Odyssey

Welcome to AI Odyssey, a new take on the traditional text adventure game where every twist in the story and each image you see is generated by artificial intelligence.

Powered by OpenAI's GPT for narrative generation and DALL-E for image creation, AI Odyssey offers a unique experience and showcases how generative AI might be used in video games

AI Odyssey is inspired by old school text adventure games.

Developed with the help of LLM:s.
