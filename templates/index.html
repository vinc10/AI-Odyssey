Welcome to AI Odyssey, an innovative text adventure game where every twist in the story and each image you see is uniquely crafted by artificial intelligence.

Powered by OpenAI's GPT for narrative generation and DALL-E for vivid image creation, AI Odyssey game offers a unique experience and showcases how AI might be used in video games

AI Odyssey is inspired by old school text adventure games.

Developed with the help of LLM:s.
